Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take for delivery of 'in stock' products?

Delivery time is 3 to 4 weeks, although it varies depending on the material requested.


Is there a minimum purchase?

Yes. Each product has a Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ).


How can I request a quotation?

Select the product you want and add it to the cart. Then enter the cart through the shopping cart icon. After that select the pricing request button to generate a quote. This is an option free of charge.

You can also contact one of our representatives by phone via e-mail.


What do I do if there is a problem with my purchase?

Please contact one of our representatives, either by phone or mail. They will advise you on how to resolve the issue.


How do I return a purchase?

Please contact one of our representatives, either by phone or mail. They will gide you step by step to finalize the process.


Do you offer discounts on big purchases?

Yes, the more products you buy, the greater the discount.


How do I make changes to my purchase order?

In 'My Account' go to Orders and in the purchase you want to modify select the configuration icon. You can also contact a representative either by phone or email.
